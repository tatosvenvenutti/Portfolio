Bird Wars

A 3rd person fixed shooter inspired by space invaders

  • Role: Gameplay Programmer
  • Team Size: 5
  • Project Length: 4 Weeks
  • Software: Visual Studio, FLECC, C++, Trello


While the first project was tough yet enjoyable, this project created a lot of stress. Making a game from scratch took a lot out of our team. As gameplay programmer my job was the implemntation of the player, enemies, and game manager. As of today this shows more of our commitment than anything the ability to crunch our project and make the most with limited time.
