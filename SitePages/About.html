Born and Raised in Ohio

Growing up, classmates would say to me, “you live in that dollhouse?” What they meant is that my mom owned a dollhouse and miniature store, and our family lived in the back. Later, my mom added a tearoom to the business, and every day about a dozen women came and worked at the store. It was a creative environment filled with inspiring people.

During undergrad, I studied video production, then eventually went on to live in Chicago. There I started a business with a friend from college and worked six years as a motion graphics artist. For a long time, I had an interest in transitioning over to video games. I started that journey by taking the online course Level Design for Video Games offered by CGMA. I then went back to school and obtained a Masters of Fine Arts in 2021.
