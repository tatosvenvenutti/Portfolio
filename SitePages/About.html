Michael Venvenutti

Born and Raised in Georgia

Born from a family in the entertainment industry, it was only natural for me to pursue my own life in this field. That field was video game development, and after playing some of my favorite games as a kid, such as Dark Souls 3, Doom 2016, Doom Eternal, and Titanfall 2, I decided I would rather create these innovative worlds and mechanics.

During my undergraduate studies, I focused on Game Development and Engine Development. I gained expertise in both the backend and frontend aspects of game development, including creating our own 3D renderer. Additionally, by working with popular engines like Unity and Unreal, I acquired skills in UX/UI design and development, as well as gameplay programming, which encompassed combat and AI design and development. My passion for improvement has driven me to continuously learn and grow, both during and after my formal education.
